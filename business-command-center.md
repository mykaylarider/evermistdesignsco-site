## Platform Strategy

### Gumroad

Use Gumroad as the main checkout hub right now because products are already live and one has proven activity.

Priorities:
- Improve product pages
- Add bundle
- Add product thumbnails/covers
- Use receipt messages for cross-selling
- Share direct product links

### Etsy

Use Etsy for search traffic once product mockups and listing images are ready.

Priorities:
- Create listings for daily planner, weekly planner, and planner bundle
- Use SEO-friendly titles
- Add 5-10 listing images per product
- Use tags around printable planner, daily planner, weekly planner, digital download, productivity printable, planner insert

### Pinterest

Use Pinterest as the evergreen traffic engine.

Priorities:
- Create pins for each product
- Pin daily for the first 14 days
- Use direct links to Gumroad or Etsy listings
- Make pins with clear product screenshots and titles

### TikTok and Instagram

Use short videos to show the problem, the printable, and the result.

Priorities:
- 10-20 second videos
- Screen recordings or printable mockups
- Hooks that speak to overwhelmed beginners and planners
- Link in bio to Gumroad storefront

### Facebook

Use Facebook for local/community traffic and groups where allowed.

Priorities:
- Post to business page
- Share helpful tips, not spam
- Use product links in comments or post body when allowed
